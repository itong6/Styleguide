The Meaty logo depicts a hamburger combined with the face of a cow, stylized in a cartoon-like manner. Meaty's whole philosophy sheds light on disconenct between the food we consume and its origins. The illustration interprets this concept in an whimsical yet literal sense by drawing a direct connection between a meat product and its animal counterpart.

LOGO CLEARSPACE

To maintain clear legibility, the logo must be surrounded with a specified clearspace margin. The margin is equal to the width of the letter "A" in the wordmark.
For logos that are not accompanied with a wordmark, the clearspace margin is equal to half the width of the icon.
