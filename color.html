We chose colors that would reflect on our design and idea, we wanted a mix of calm colors and more intense colors. Having that mix creates a balance of clash color harmony, we will create contrast with the light colors by using them more broad and darker colors defined and detailed.

Dune: This will be our text color. Slightly lighter than black, gives less stress while reading while still having contrast.

Falu Red: This color represents the blood and violence of our design and concept, it reflects on the real world problem we are bringing awareness to. This color will also be used for our button because it repreesnts proceeding to the next task.

Bleach White: This color will be used as part of the background, it’s a calm and dairy like color which represents our idea of using farm animals.

White Smoke: This color will be used as part of the background, we didn’t want just white as it would have created more contrast than we wanted. White Smoke is easier on the eyes and is a similar color to some of our graphics being used.

Harvest Gold: This color will be used for the menu frame when users are deciding what animal and method they want to choose. This color represents the fences on a farm to resemble the feelings of a farm.
