We selected colours that would reflect on our design and idea. We wanted a mix of calm colours and more intense colors. Having that mix creates a balance of clash colour harmony. By using these colours, we can create contrast with the light colors by using them against bolder and darker colours.

Dune: Used as our primary colour for text. Slightly lighter than black, this colour induces less stress while reading, while still maintaining high-contrast.

Falu Red: Used only for essential/important tasks, such as call-to-actions and buttons. Inspired by the colour of blood, this colour reflects the graphic aspect of animal product harvesting, which is the issue Meaty is bring awareness to.

Bleach White: Used as an alternate media colour/background. This colour is used to evoke a sense of calmness. Inspired of dairy products, keeping with the theme of animal products.

White Smoke: Used as a neutral media colour. This colour will be primary seen in the background, creating whitespace in the application.

Harvest Gold: Used for the menu frame when users are selecting thier animal and method of choice. This colour was inspired from fences on a farms, keeping with the barn animal theme.
